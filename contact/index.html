Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for best model availability, though we can accommodate rush projects when possible.

Do you work internationally?

Yes! Our models are available for international projects. Travel and accommodation arrangements can be discussed during project planning.

What's included in model rates?

Our rates include the model's time, basic hair and makeup, and usage rights for agreed-upon media. Additional services can be arranged as needed.
